# Assumptions

All content, numbers, and claims in this proposal site are **directional** and predicated on the following assumptions. Each is explicit so that ipNX reviewers can validate or challenge them before the Design Phase kicks off.

## Data access
- **No internal ipNX data access** — DSG has not been granted access to ipNX systems, financial ledgers, subscriber base, BSS/OSS, network reports, or CRM records at the time of proposal authoring.
- All revenue uplift, ARPU, and churn-reduction ranges are **industry benchmarks** (10–15% ARPU uplift, 5–10% churn reduction) drawn from comparable ISP-to-MVNO transitions in Europe, Asia, and Africa — not ipNX-specific projections.
- Nigerian market statistics (200M+ mobile subscriptions, double-digit data growth) are public / regulator-sourced figures and are used illustratively.

## Strategic framing
- The proposal articulates a **directional strategy**, not a signed commercial plan. The granular 60-month business model, target segments, bundle economics, and MNO host-agreement terms are Design Phase outputs — not inputs.
- The choice of floLIVE as the IoT platform is a **recommended** partnership, not a contracted one. Alternative IoT MVNE platforms can be evaluated during Design.
- Host MNO (MTN / Airtel / Glo / 9mobile) is **unspecified** — selection follows Design Phase commercial modelling.

## Regulatory & compliance
- The site references NCC and NDPR at a conceptual level. Formal regulatory opinion, licensing pathways (MVNO licence / numbering range), and KYC / lawful-intercept design are Design Phase deliverables.
- Data-sovereignty architecture (local breakout, in-country CMP deployment) is presented as **available capability**, not as a regulator-approved design.

## Commercials
- USD 250,000 fixed fee covers DSG's DBOT services only. **Platform fees (floLIVE CMP, core, SIM, eSIM, local-breakout), host-MNO wholesale, KYC, devices, working capital, and launch marketing are explicitly excluded** and sized during Design.
- Pricing is indicative and subject to USD FX fluctuation (per Terms & Conditions, §18 of the proposal).

## Validation gates
Before any commitment is treated as binding:
1. Signed Scope of Work.
2. Steering Committee nomination.
3. Baseline data exchange (financials, operations, subscriber base).
4. Design Phase kick-off workshop (within 14 days of approval).
5. Design Phase deliverables (MVP, GTM, OM, ICX, BM) reviewed and accepted by ipNX Exco.

## Non-goals of the website
- The site is **not** a substitute for the signed contract — it is a communication artefact.
- The site does **not** capture confidential ipNX data; forms (if added) route to DSG inboxes only, never to public endpoints.
